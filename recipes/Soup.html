Vegan Soup

Description

Quick and easy vegan soup that tastes so good, you won’t even realize it’s loaded with nutritious veggies.

This soup is so good, we could eat it every day.

Ingredients

  • Pasta called fideo
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic powder
  • Herbs and spices
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix a little piece of onion, tomatoes and water in a blender.
  2. Put in on a stove.
  3. Add the pasta.
  4. Wait until boil and add some herbs and spices.
  5. The dish is ready to sirve, enjoy it.
