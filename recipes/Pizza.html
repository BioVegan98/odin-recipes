Vegan Pizza

Description

Quick delicious and easy vegan pizza that tastes so good, you won’t even realize it’s loaded with nutritious veggies.

This pizza is so good, we could eat it every day.

Ingredients

  • Dough, better if its fresh
  • Vegetables
  • Onion, garlic and tomato paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs and spices
  • Mushroom powder

Steps

  1. Chop everything and put it on the dough.
  2. Put it on the oven and wait until is ready to eat.
  3. Enjoy it.
